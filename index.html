Undergraduate Research

The Impact of Hurricane Katrina on Crime in Louisiana

In this paper, I investigate the effect that the notorious Hurricane Katrina had on crime in Louisiana using a difference-in-difference (DD) strategy. I find that burglary, larceny, and robbery rates all increased after Katrina, while other crime rates were not affected.
